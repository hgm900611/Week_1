    Dynamic TCP Acknowledgement and Other Stories about e/(e-1)

    This paper leaves me a very good impression in the sense that the proposed algorithm and mathematical analysis is pretty beautiful.

    It mainly focuses on the Dynamic TCP Acknowledgement issue aimed at minimizing the acknowledgement cost and presents a randomized algorithm which achieves the competitive ratio e/(e-1). It turns out that this ratio is completely the same as the 'ski-rental' problem.
